VEGAN BANANA BREAD RECIPE

  • 4 small or 3 large overripe bananas
  • 210 g whole wheat flour
  • 75 g organic pure cane sugar (or 1/2 finely chopped dates)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 75 ml neutral flavored oil (or coconut oil in liquid state, vegan butter at room temp or applesauce)
  • 56 ml almond milk, use only if needed
  • STEP 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease your loaf pan.
  • STEP 2: In a medium/large size bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt, set aside.
  • STEP 3: In a medium bowl, mash bananas. Add the oil, vanilla and bananas to the dry ingredients and mix until combined, do not overmix. If mixture seems too thick, add the almond milk.
